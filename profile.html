profile

Erik has had the desire to be an architect for as long as he can remember. Growing up in the city of Toronto, he was filled with awe and inspiration at the urban spaces and buildings of the city.

He received his Bachelor of Architecture degree as well as a Bachelor of Environmental Studies from the University of Waterloo, Canada. He has practiced in a diverse range of places, including Canada, Italy, Australia, and the United States.

His portfolio includes commercial, retail, public and education projects.

Erik has been a member of the Ontario Association of Architects, Board of Architects Queensland, Australian Institute of Architects, and the Green Building Council of Australia.
